# **App Name**: Bandar Asri

## Core Features:

- Beranda: Display introductory information about Desa Bandar, key highlights, and quick links to other sections.
- Profil Desa: Provide a detailed overview of Desa Bandar, including its history, geography, demographics, and administrative structure.
- Berita CRUD: Enable administrators to create, read, update, and delete news articles related to Desa Bandar.
- Potensi Desa: Showcase the economic, natural, and cultural potential of Desa Bandar, attracting investment and tourism.
- Galeri: Display a visual collection of images and videos showcasing the beauty, activities, and events of Desa Bandar.
- Kontak: Provide contact information for Desa Bandar's administrative office and key personnel.
- Admin Authentication: Secure the backend CMS with Firebase Authentication, ensuring only authorized personnel can manage the website's content.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) evoking tranquility and nature.
- Background color: Very light greenish-blue (#E0EEEE), a muted shade of the primary, creates a calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Turquoise (#40E0D0) draws attention to calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for a modern yet approachable feel.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent different sections and features of the website.
- A clean, grid-based layout to present content in an organized and visually appealing manner.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience and provide visual feedback.
